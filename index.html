3D CHARACTER

A 3D character refers to a digital model of a character that is created using 3D computer graphics software. This type of character is often used in video games, movies, and other forms of digital media. 3D characters are typically created by digital artists who use specialized software to create a model of the character, which includes all the details such as the shape of the body, facial features, and clothing. The model can then be animated and used in various media. One of the advantages of using 3D characters is that they can be manipulated and viewed from any angle, making them very versatile for use in different types of media.
